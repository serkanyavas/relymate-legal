# Privacy Policy for Relymate

**Last Updated: November 6, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Relymate ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information (Optional)**
- Email address (if you create an account)
- Name or username (if provided)
- Profile information (if provided)

**Conversation Data**
- Text conversations you paste for analysis
- Screenshots you upload (temporarily processed)
- Analysis results and history (if you choose to save)

**Purchase Information**
- In-app purchase history
- Token balance and usage
- Subscription status (if applicable)

### Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- App version

**Usage Data**
- Features used
- Analysis frequency
- App interactions
- Error logs and crash reports

**Analytics Data**
- Anonymous usage statistics
- Performance metrics
- Feature popularity

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Services**
   - Process conversation analysis
   - Generate AI-powered insights
   - Store your analysis history (optional)
   - Process in-app purchases

2. **Improve Our App**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and errors
   - Develop new features
   - Optimize performance

3. **Communication**
   - Send service updates
   - Respond to support requests
   - Notify about new features
   - Send promotional offers (with consent)

4. **Security**
   - Prevent fraud and abuse
   - Protect user accounts
   - Ensure data security
   - Comply with legal obligations

## Data Processing and AI

**OpenAI Integration**
- Conversations are sent to OpenAI's API for analysis
- OpenAI processes data according to their privacy policy
- Data is not used to train OpenAI models (per API terms)
- Conversations are not stored by OpenAI beyond processing

**Our Processing**
- Analysis results are stored on secure servers
- Conversation text is temporarily cached for processing
- Screenshots are automatically deleted after 24 hours
- You can delete your data anytime

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

**Service Providers**
- OpenAI (for AI analysis)
- Supabase (for data storage)
- RevenueCat (for purchase processing)
- Analytics services (anonymized data only)

**Legal Requirements**
- When required by law
- To protect our rights
- To prevent fraud or abuse
- In response to legal requests

**Business Transfers**
- In case of merger or acquisition
- Users will be notified of any changes

## Data Storage and Security

**Security Measures**
- End-to-end encryption for data transmission
- Secure cloud storage (Supabase)
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication

**Data Retention**
- Analysis history: Until you delete it
- Screenshots: Automatically deleted after 24 hours
- Account data: Until account deletion
- Anonymous analytics: Retained indefinitely

**Data Location**
- Data is stored on servers in the United States
- Complies with international data protection standards

## Your Rights and Choices

**Access and Control**
- View your analysis history
- Export your data
- Delete specific analyses
- Delete your entire account

**Privacy Settings**
- Opt out of analytics
- Disable history saving
- Control data sharing preferences
- Manage notification preferences

**Account Deletion**
- Request account deletion anytime
- All personal data will be permanently deleted
- Process completes within 30 days
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance

## Children's Privacy

Relymate is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.
