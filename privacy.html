Privacy Policy for Relymate

Last Updated: November 6, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Relymate ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Account Information (Optional)

  • Email address (if you create an account)
  • Name or username (if provided)
  • Profile information (if provided)

Conversation Data

  • Text conversations you paste for analysis
  • Screenshots you upload (temporarily processed)
  • Analysis results and history (if you choose to save)

Purchase Information

  • In-app purchase history
  • Token balance and usage
  • Subscription status (if applicable)

Automatically Collected Information

Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version

Usage Data

  • Features used
  • Analysis frequency
  • App interactions
  • Error logs and crash reports

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  1. Provide Services - Process conversation analysis, generate AI-powered insights, store your analysis history (optional), process in-app purchases
  2. Improve Our App - Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs and errors, develop new features, optimize performance
  3. Communication - Send service updates, respond to support requests, notify about new features
  4. Security - Prevent fraud and abuse, protect user accounts, ensure data security, comply with legal obligations

Data Processing and AI

OpenAI Integration

  • Conversations are sent to OpenAI's API for analysis
  • OpenAI processes data according to their privacy policy
  • Data is not used to train OpenAI models (per API terms)
  • Conversations are not stored by OpenAI beyond processing

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers - OpenAI (for AI analysis), Supabase (for data storage), RevenueCat (for purchase processing)
  • Legal Requirements - When required by law, to protect our rights, to prevent fraud or abuse

Data Storage and Security

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage (Supabase)
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

Data Retention

  • Analysis history: Until you delete it
  • Screenshots: Automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • Account data: Until account deletion

Your Rights and Choices

  • View your analysis history
  • Export your data
  • Delete specific analyses
  • Delete your entire account

Children's Privacy

Relymate is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy:

Email: support@relymate.app

Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
